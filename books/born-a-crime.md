# Born a Crime

Author: Trevor Noah

## Overview
A memoir by comedian Trevor Noah describing his childhood growing up in apartheid and post-apartheid South Africa.

## Key Idea
Humor and resilience can coexist even in the most difficult social and political circumstances.

## My Takeaways

• Identity is shaped by environment and language  
• Humor can be a survival mechanism  
• Systems shape opportunities more than talent
