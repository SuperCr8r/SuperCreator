<!-- ========================= SUMMARY ========================= -->
<section id="summary" class="accordion-section">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-1"><span>Summary</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Brief Summary</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>The Midnight Library follows Nora Seed, a woman filled with regrets who finds herself in a liminal library between life and death. Each book represents a life she could have lived if different choices were made. Guided by a librarian and confronting versions of herself, Nora navigates regrets, learns empathy, and reevaluates the meaning of fulfillment and purpose. The novel blends speculative conceit with emotional introspection to ask whether a perfect life exists and how to accept the imperfect one.</p>
</div>
</div>

</div>
</section>

<!-- ========================= CHAPTER-WISE SUMMARY ========================= -->
<section id="chapters" class="accordion-section">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-1"><span>Chapter-wise Summary</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 1 — The End</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>Nora experiences a deep nadir in her life, feeling overwhelmed by perceived failures and isolating herself. The chapter establishes her regrets, relationships, and the emotional landscape that precipitates the story's inciting incident.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 2 — The Midnight Library</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>Nora awakens in a strange library between life and death, where endless shelves hold books of possible lives. She meets the librarian, who explains the premise: Nora can explore alternate versions of her life to see how different choices play out.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 3 — The First Choices</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>Nora opens her first alternate life, experiencing a reality where she pursued a relationship or career she abandoned. She notices small differences and the emotional consequences of seemingly simple decisions.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 4 — Success and Its Hollow</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>In a life where Nora achieved professional success, she confronts the emptiness success can bring without meaningful connection. The chapter complicates the notion that external achievements guarantee happiness.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 5 — Relationships Reimagined</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>Nora explores lives with different romantic and familial ties, recognizing both missed joys and hidden costs. She gains empathy for others and a clearer sense of her own values through these interactions.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 6 — Ordinary Joys</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>Here Nora experiences simple, everyday pleasures—friendships, music, small acts of kindness—that reveal fulfillment does not require grand gestures. The narrative emphasizes presence and appreciation.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 7 — Confronting Regret</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>Nora wrestles with the persistent pangs of regret and learns to reframe mistakes as part of a life’s texture rather than definitive failures. She begins to understand acceptance and forgiveness.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 8 — The Librarian's Lessons</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>The librarian offers philosophical guidance about choices, possibility, and the limits of hypothetical lives. Nora's journey becomes as much internal—reassessing identity—as it is external exploration.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 9 — A Return to Self</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>After trying numerous lives, Nora appreciates the factors that shaped her core character—resilience, compassion, curiosity—and contemplates what she truly wants from life moving forward.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-subsection">
<button class="accordion-toggle level-2"><span>Chapter 10 — Choosing to Live</span></button>
<div class="accordion-content">
<p>In the conclusion Nora makes a pivotal choice about returning to her life with renewed perspective. The ending emphasizes acceptance, hope, and the commitment to seek meaning in an imperfect existence.</p>
</div>
</div>
