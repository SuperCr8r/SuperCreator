<div class="accordion-content">

<p><strong>The Happiness Advantage</strong> argues that happiness is not a result of success but a driver of it. A positive mental state enhances cognitive performance, resilience, and decision-making.</p>

<p>The book introduces 7 core principles that help individuals rewire their brains toward positivity, enabling higher productivity and better outcomes in work and life.</p>
